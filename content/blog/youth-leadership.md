# Youth Leadership in Media Advocacy

## The Power of the Next Generation
Africa has the youngest population in the world. This is not just a demographic fact; it is our greatest asset in the fight against climate change. 

![Youth Workshop](/images/gallery/IMG_2021.JPG)

### Why Youth?
Young people are more open to adopting *new technologies* and *digital platforms*. They are also the most motivated because it is their future at stake.

> "We are not just leaders of tomorrow; we are active problem solvers of today."

### Building Digital Literacy
Digital literacy is the bridge to modern climate solutions. Our programs focus on:
- **GIS Mapping**: Using satellite data to track reforestation.
- **Mobile Apps**: Building tools for community reporting of climate impacts.
